Our #Fordistas Gallery in Wynwood

The Fordistas platform has always sought to include both local and international talent. The Fordistas Residency Program has commissioned over 60+ renowned artists to live, work and be inspired by South Florida’s cultures and communities. At the #Fordistas gallery, located in the Heart of Miami,Fl, showcases, exhibitions and experiments for artists live here. In the spirit of connecting the community and our cultural program, we've connected artist communites all throughout South Florida.
