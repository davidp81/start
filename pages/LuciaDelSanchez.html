Lucia Del Sanchez

Sanchez tells her story with a textural mimicry in sculpture, painting and photography.

Avenrurando

Lucia Del Sanchez is an artist and designer based in Miami, FL. Of Cuban and Slovenian decent, Sanchez tells her story with a textural mimicry in sculpture, painting, and photography. Manifesting observations of South Florida and other peculiar and beautiful spaces, she creates multimedia artwork that questions authenticity and breaks from the historical narrative. Doing so with an ear to tourism, ecology and visual culture, her practice is a reflection of its surroundings

Cypress Signals

Sanchez received her BFA from The Cooper Union School of Art in 2015. She also studied for one year at the Gerrit Rietveld Academie in the Netherlands. Now based in her hometown of Miami Beach, Lucia runs an alternative arts venue in the Design District with father and collaborator Oliver Sanchez.

Look Both Ways

Lucia Del Sanchez is a Young Arts alumni who has shown work at The Margullies Collection, Diana Lowenstein Gallery, Superchief Gallery at Aqua Art Fair, De Brakke Grond(NL), Bridewell Studios(UK), Chinatown Soup (NYC), and is currently in an exhibition at The Bakehouse Art Complex in Wynwood.

Submission Guidelines

We accept submissions only through our website submission system. We do not accept submissions through postal services or email. You may send us PDFs (under 10mb) including pictures of your art work and biography. If you do not have your work in PDF format, you may include a link to your works in the message box.

Links to personal websites, Instagram, Behance, etc... will all be accepted. PDF is not required. Please include your full name and email along with submission.
