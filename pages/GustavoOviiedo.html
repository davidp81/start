Gustavo Oviedo

Diver, nautical researcher, and multidisciplinary artist.

Underwater Photography

Diver, nautical researcher, and multidisciplinary artist, Gustavo Oviedo has worked in Miami for the past 20 years. Oviedo was born in Paris, France and raised between Colombia, Venezuela, Mexico and finally Miami.

Ocean Photography

He has a Master’s Degree in Visual Effects and has painted murals around the world for the past two decades.

Stiltsville Bottles Exhibition

He works in various mediums including video, photography, collage, sculpture and muralism. Oviedo’s work has been exhibited at the Art Center / South Florida, Young at Art Museum, Art and Culture center of Hollywood, Boca Museum of Art, among others.

Submission Guidelines

We accept submissions only through our website submission system. We do not accept submissions through postal services or email. You may send us PDFs (under 10mb) including pictures of your art work and biography. If you do not have your work in PDF format, you may include a link to your works in the message box.

Links to personal websites, Instagram, Behance, etc... will all be accepted. PDF is not required. Please include your full name and email along with submission.
